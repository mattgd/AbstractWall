Gradient Rules
 - 1/6 turn of the color wheel
 - Color options: black, blue, cyan, dark_gray, gray, green, light_gray, magenta, orange, pink, red, white, yellow
 - Gradient options:
	- blue > magenta
 	- magenta > pink
 	- pink > red
 	- red > orange
 	- orange > yellow
 	- yellow > green
 	- green > cyan
 	- cyan > blue
